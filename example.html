My trip to Avengers Campus

I went to California for a get away with my family. We ended up going to Avengers Campus and it was so fun. Avengers Campus is part of Disney World. It was a lot of fun I went wth all my family and got to see all the Avengers. I ended up meeting a lot of the avengers ! My family told me they weren't the real deal but I knew Tom Holland was under that spiderman suit. Every ride we went on was so much fun! My favorite was the new Guardians of the Galaxy ride. It had the Ultron duplicate from age of ultron and the ride was so realistic. It had every scene from guardians of the galaxy and I knew all of them. My family thought it was very weird how obsessed I was with the whole area but I highly recommend. I can't think of anything wrong with the place and if you love marvel movies I 100% recommend. They even had a euro stand from the first avengers, which I higly reccomend getting because it was so delicous. They also had a Pim Cafe, which had small and large portions of food it was so cool and authentic. The food was great too but it was really expensive. The one downside was the spiderman ride line was extremely long, the ride kept breaking down. Once we got on the ride though it was so cool. I kept thinking about how much programming there most of been for the ride. It was like shooting webs and different enemies and you didn't even need a gun, all with your own hands.

Pros

  • The Whole Trip and all the Rides
  • The food
  • Meeting and seeing all the Avengers

Cons

  • The long lines
  • None of my family watched Marvel Movies
  • My family making me go on rides outside of Avengers Campus
